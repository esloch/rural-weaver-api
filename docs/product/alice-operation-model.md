# Alice Operation Model — Delícias da Roça Pilot

The operation is organized in weekly cycles. Each campaign has a delivery date, available products, limited stock, pickup points, delivery zones, payment instructions, manual confirmation and producer settlement.

Current modeled rules:

```text
orders on Sunday and Thursday
closing time: 20:00
minimum order: R$ 50.00
minimum order exception: Floricultura Pauli pickup
manual confirmation required
payment proof required
```

Seeded pickup points: Papelaria Limas, Floricultura Pauli, Armazém Campeche Produtos Naturais, Adoça e Confeita, Condomínio Arquipélago and UFSC - DCE.

Seeded delivery zones: São Pedro de Alcântara, São José/Palhoça, Florianópolis Continente and Florianópolis Ilha.

Seeded payment methods: PIX, Depósito bancário/transferência, Boleto à vista and Link de pagamento com cartão.

Seeded CSA plans: Cesta Média Unitária, Cesta Grande Unitária, Assinatura Quinzenal Média, Assinatura Semanal Média, Assinatura Quinzenal Grande and Assinatura Semanal Grande.
