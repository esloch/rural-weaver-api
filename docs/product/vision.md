# Product Vision

Rural Weaver / Tejido Rural is a digital platform for campaign-based rural and agroecological operations. The first pilot is based on Delícias da Roça and Alice's real workflow.

The goal is to gradually replace forms, manual messages, spreadsheets, payment checks and manual producer settlements with a central platform for weekly campaigns, orders, stock, pickup/delivery, CSA plans, payments, financial summaries, producer settlements and CSV exports.
